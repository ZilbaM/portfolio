Overview

As a major project of my second year of study, the students are put in a real life situation and have to realize a project for a company. The client i had to work with needed a showcase website for his web agency.

Technical process

As a contraint from the client, we used the CMS Wordpress. I never really used it before, just learned the basis and the creation of a child theme. Hence, i had to do a lot of research on how to develop an entire website. Once i got used to the general use and the different mechanics of Wordpress, it went smooth and easy. As my first experience with a CMS, it went quite well ! I used a few plugins, played with the different Wordpress functions, and it was really pleasant.
